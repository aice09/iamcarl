introvert

2022-11-30
0 minute read
life

I think this is me:

I’m an introvert. I’m not shy and I’m not stuck. I’m just listening and observing. I’m very straightforward and you know that. I can’t stand small talks, but if I find something that interest me, I can talk about it for hours. I really, really enjoy my own company, so if I open up to you, it just means that you’re special. So please! Don’t miss that opportunity.

– diimejii
